How do we work?

Communication

Communication is one of the most important parts of software development. At Two Angry Developers we focus heavily on communication with our clients to ensure that we deliver quality software up to the customers specifications. We utilize the principles of Agile Software Development, delivering functionality on increments and relying on robust planning to continuously improve our work flow process.

Development

The development process here at Two Angry Developers is efficient and revolutionary. We deliver functionality on small increments keeping the customer up to date with our work. This constant interaction with our client ensures that we have working software satisfying the requirements. We are very responsive to change even if it is late in the development, the flexibility in our work ensures customer satisfaction. If the client is happy, we are happy.

Quality

Here at Two Angry Developers quality is one of our main goals. We focus on using specific techniques unique to our trade, such as test-driven development, continuous integration and design patterns. These techniques are key to increasing the quality of our software and enhancing the product development process. We have trained Quality Assurance engineers specifically focused on maintaining the reliability and correctness of the product.
